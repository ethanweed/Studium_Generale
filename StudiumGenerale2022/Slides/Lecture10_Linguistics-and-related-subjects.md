## Vocabulary acquisition

<div id = "left">

Word-learning rate:

- Babies: 0 words
- 9 words per day between age 2 and 6
- Websters dictionary: approx. 50,000 words
- Adult receptive vocabulary: approx. 17,000 words (Goulden, Nation and Read, 1990)
