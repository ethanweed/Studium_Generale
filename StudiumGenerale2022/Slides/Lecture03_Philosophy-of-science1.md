## Species of syllogism


 <div id = "left_border_small">

_Modus ponens_

If Ethan is awake, he will drink coffee.  

Ethan is drinking coffee.  

.˙. Ethan is awake

</div>

<div id = "right_border_small">

_Modus tollens_  

If Ethan is awake, he will drink coffee.  

Ethan is _not_ drinking coffee.  

.˙. Ethan is _not_ awake.

</div>
